About Onesmos Nesib Academy

Located in Nekemte, Oromia, Ethiopia, Onesmos Nesib Academy offers quality education for primary and secondary students. Our mission is to nurture young minds with modern curriculum blended with national standards, committed teachers, and a vibrant student community.

Founded with the vision to serve thousands of students, our academy integrates technology and innovation for a future-ready education.

Our Values

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Inclusivity and diversity
  • Student-centered approach
  • Continuous innovation
  • Community engagement
